Wordsearch

This is a tool to search for five letter words! (Primarily for use with Wordle). For words that are not five letters, that functionality is still in research and development. Do note that this search will not work on Internet Explorer due to a few "newer features". I have also notice that it does not work on some mobile browsers, if that is the case, try updating your browser app or try using desktop / laptop instead.

Directions: Type any single letter you know in the top five blue boxes. If you don't know the letter, place a * (asterisk / star) in its place. For letters you know are included, but you don't know where, put them in the bottom left green box. And letters that you know are not in the word in the bottom right red box. Press "search" to search and "reset" to reset the form.
