Photo Editing

Note: the following is all done in the latest version of Adobe Lightroom Classic (screenshots from version 12.5). I will also occasionally use Adobe Photoshop as well.



My "starting" preset, (which you can download here) basically adds the things I always forget to update, it does not change contrast or color or anything. Basically:
- checks the "Constrain to Image" box in the Crop Tool
- adds evenly split points to the Tone Curve (shadows, highlights, etc.)
- sets Luminance to "10" and Color to "100" in the Noise Reduction tab
- sets Grain Amount to "10" and Roughness to "20" in the Effects tab
- checks the "Remove Chromatic Aberration" box in the Lens Corrections tab
